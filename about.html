About Us

We are the oldest gasket rubber manufacturing company in country.

The founder of SS Rubber Industries is (Late) Hazi Abdul Mannan. Today SS Rubber has become a symbol of trust more than a word to its customers mind. But the path to come in this level wasn’t very easy at all. The journey of SS Rubber started back in 90’s decade. After one decade of inception, the elder son of founder, Fazla Shahnewaz joined as deputy managing director (DMD). At his tenure, company experienced a huge prosperity both in market enlargement and technological advancement. Later on, after the passing of Hazi Abdul Mannan, Fazla Shahnewaz took his position and still running the company with full sincerity. Furthermore, in last decade, Minhazul Abedin, the younger son of Hazi Abdul Mannan joined in the organization as DMD.
From the very beginning, SS Rubber is maintaining zero tolerance policy for quality. Apart from product quality development currently the organization is focusing on product modernization. To ensure the quality of the product every year the organization is testing its product in BUET. The lifeblood of this organization is its customers. That’s why “Thinking Straight” for the customers is the fundamental ideology of this company.
